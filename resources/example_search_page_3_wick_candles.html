About 3-Wick Candles from Bath & Body Works

What’s the easiest way to turn your house into a home? The World’s Best 3-Wick Candles. We could list tons of reasons why, but here are our top five:

  • Amazing room-filling fragrances
  • Consistent quality from beginning to end
  • Delivers up to 45 hours of fragrance
  • Over 50 exclusive fragrances
  • Every candle is 100% money-back guaranteed!

It’s no wonder we’ve developed a bit of a cult following. Candles are the perfect partner for cozying up on chilly days, hanging out warm summer nights and entertaining company during every dinner party of the year. And with so many super-cute packaging options to choose from, our candles always make your home look amazing.

Where are the best places to put candles, you ask? Coffee tables, dining rooms, kitchens, patio tables, counters and islands – basically anywhere you need a fragrance boost or pop of style. Speaking of style…candle accessories are a total must to complete the look. From glitzy holders to modern pedestals, there’s a match for each room.

P.S. The World’s Best 3-Wick Candles also make the ultimate gifts…for every person, every occasion and every reason. You can’t go wrong.
